Stats are separated into 3 categories:

## Physical
Physical traits represent things like lifting capacity, your ability to deftly maneuver tools or weapons deftly, and your capability to withstand hits or poison.
## Mental
Mental traits represent things like your memory fidelity, capability to learn new things, and your ability to maintain concentration through distractions. 
## Beyond
Beyond traits represent normally unquantifiable traits like your love for your favorite band, your ability to captivate a room, or just plain luck.
